DIRECTORS NOTE

WELCOME

We welcome you to the White Dove Schools. The White Dove Schools provide an outstanding all-round education of international standard.


GOAL

We are committed to the pursuit of individual excellence with emphasis on discipline, leadership training, high academic standards, moral and ethical values based on Christian principles.


HISTORY

The White Dove Schools was founded on the 9th of April, 2004 by a crop of vision-driven educationists and seasoned administrators.


FUNCTION

We run day and boarding facilities for both girls and boys between the ages of 2 and 18 years incorporating kindergarten, nursery, primary and secondary. We continue to operate a policy that has continued to entrench good academic standards and enduring moral values.

Do come in, you are welcome!
